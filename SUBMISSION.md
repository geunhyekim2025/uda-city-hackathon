# UDA-city heat-risk — hackathon submission

**Live site:** https://geunhyekim2025.github.io/uda-city-hackathon/

A SUEWS heat-hazard layer for ten neighbourhoods of UDA-city, bridged to a
socio-economic heat-**risk** indicator, with an honest account of where that
bridge holds and where it breaks.

## Headline

The **hottest** neighbourhood (Jade Gardens — a low-rise green periphery) is
**not** the **highest-risk** one (Kampong Lama — a dense informal settlement,
only 3rd-hottest). Heat is a hazard; risk is what that hazard does to exposed,
vulnerable people. This holds across humid-heat metrics and a +2.5 K future.

## Reproduce

```bash
uv venv --python 3.12 .venv
uv pip install --python .venv "supy>=2026.6.5" pyyaml matplotlib pandas
.venv/bin/python scripts/smoke_test.py          # asserts T2/QH finite (sanity)
.venv/bin/python scripts/reproduce.py           # 3 SUEWS scenarios -> CSVs + 6 figures
```

Deterministic. Present / future dangerous-heat-hour totals: **242 / 1861**;
intervention reductions (present): **−60 / −65 / −73 %**. Under the +2.5 °C
future the same intervention offsets only **36–43 %** of the warming-driven rise
— adaptation helps but does not erase a hotter climate (`adapt and mitigate`).

## Method (one paragraph)

SUEWS via `supy 2026.6.5` (NARP net radiation + classic OHM storage heat;
single-layer, laptop-runnable). **Hazard** = hours with hourly-mean 2 m air
temperature (T2) above 35 °C after a 14-day spin-up (humid variant: wet-bulb,
Stull 2011, above 28 °C from the model's own T2 + RH2). **Risk** = UNDRR-style
`hazard × exposure × vulnerability`, each min–max scaled and combined as a
geometric mean (`risk_bridge.build_risk`, used verbatim). The intervention is a
surface re-skin only — built form and physics unchanged — and the config was
validated (`suews validate`, zero errors) before running.

## Honest limits

Environmental hazard, not a health outcome; the socio-economic layer is
**synthetic** (read ranks, not absolute values); min–max scaling is **relative**
(it hides absolute progress and pins sparsely-populated areas to zero risk);
district means hide individuals; the future is a pseudo-warming stress test;
greening cooling assumes the vegetation is kept watered. See the site's
"where it holds / where it breaks" section and `risk_bridge.md`.
